Power Yoga

Power yoga is an athletic style of yoga that combines the physicality of traditional yoga with the energy and intensity of a yoga. Often taught in heated rooms, it focuses on building strength, core stability, and cardio. Get ready to feel the burn and break a sweat!

Meditation Class

Embark on a journey of inner stillness and self-discovery in our meditation class. Guided by a compassionate facilitator, you’ll learn to quiet the mind, deepen your breath, and find peace amidst life’s hustle. Join us as we create a sacred space for healing and presence.

Aerobics Class

Aerobic classes, also known as cardio yoga, elevate your heart rate and breathing over an extended period. These dynamic exercises engage large muscle groups, improve cardiovascular endurance, boost lung capacity, and promote overall heart health.

Spiritual Yoga

Spiritual yoga goes beyond physical postures; it’s a journey of inner connection. Rooted in ancient philosophy, it unites body, mind, and spirit. Through breath, meditation, and mindful movement, it reveals our interconnectedness with the universe.

Cardio Training

Cardio Yoga is a type of yoga class that combines Yoga moves with cardiovascular exercises. The yoga takes classic yoga moves and makes them a bit faster, with a more rapid flow between sequences. The non-stop movement gets your heart beating and your muscles burning.
